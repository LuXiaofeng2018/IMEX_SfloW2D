Benchmark test BM1 from Cordonnier et al. (2015).
In this test, an initial reservoir of isoviscous Newtonian ‘lava’ is released and spreads on a flat surface. Lava is not interacting thermally with its surroundings. The fluid has a density of 2700 kg/m3 and a dynamic viscosity of 10^4 [Pa s].


REFERENCES:
Benoit BJR Cordonnier; Einat Lev; Fanny Garel (2014), "BENCHMARKING LAVA FLOWS -BM1- DAM BREAK FLOW," https://vhub.org/resources/3479.
